Coaching for Entrance Tests

NDA (National Defence Academy): Our NDA coaching program is designed to help students clear the entrance exam with flying colors. We cover all subjects, including Mathematics, General Ability Test (GAT).

AFCAT (Air Force Common Admission Test): We offer comprehensive AFCAT coaching, including preparation for all sections of the exam: General Awareness, Verbal Ability, Numerical Ability, Reasoning, and more. Our instructors guide you through the entire process, ensuring you’re well-prepared for the written and interview stages.

SSC (Staff Selection Commission): Our SSC coaching is focused on preparing students for exams like SSC CGL, SSC CHSL, and SSC GD. We cover all subjects in detail, including Quantitative Aptitude, General Intelligence, English Comprehension, and General Awareness, along with effective test-taking strategies.
